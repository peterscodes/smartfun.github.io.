Smart Fun Limited has an existing agricultural projects with investment in poultry farm with 4500 layers and 3000 broilers, 6 acres of plantain farm and now proposing to establish 20 hectares of pineapple farm in Abeokuta, Ogun state. In Nigeria, Pineapple is an FMCG fruit with high returns on investment and grows mainly in “Evergreen Savanna”, but most local farmers within the region does not have the financial capacity to cultivate pineapple in large quantity due to its high capital required. There is a very high local market demand for pineapple, most especially the “Smooth Cayenne” while major fruit juice and wine companies are also on the search for reliable and consistent supplier of the produce. Internationally, pineapple is a product that is legally allowed to be traded at the international commodity market. Hence, this brings about another reliable market source for the produce. A piece of “Smooth Cayenne” pineapple costs N200 to N500 in the market depending on the weights, while we are considering a market conservative price of N150 per piece. 5 In Nigeria, pineapple is used by fruit juice, pharmaceutical, trado-medical practitioners, and food industries as its bark, crown and other parts are useful as medicine for the treatment of ailments. Hence, Smart Fun Limited will invest into Production of pineapple for consistent supply to local and international markets. Our Poultry farm of 4500 layers and 3000 boilers is also among the Nation highly agricultural establishments.
